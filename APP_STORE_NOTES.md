# App Store Notes

## App Name
RBT Varpa Rain

## Subtitle
Varpa planner and results

## Category
Sports

## Promotional Text
Plan Varpa matches, record measured throws, manage kit, and learn the history and rules of the Gotland precision sport.

## Description
RBT Varpa Rain is a full local companion for the Swedish precision throwing sport varpa. It helps players understand the sport, plan matches, record measured results, compare best throws, and keep notes on equipment and playing conditions.

Features:

- Match planner with opponent, venue, date, format, surface, weather, target distance, and notes.
- Result journal for player and opponent throw distances.
- Automatic best throw, average miss, and win/chase summaries.
- Varpa kit list with material, weight, diameter, notes, and favorite equipment.
- Almanac with sport history, rules, unique characteristics, and glossary.
- Offline use with no account, ads, or tracking.

## Review Notes
RBT Varpa Rain is fully offline. No login is required. The app stores only local plans, results, kit notes, and profile values with UserDefaults and includes a reset option in Profile.
